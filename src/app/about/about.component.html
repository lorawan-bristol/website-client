Bristol LoRaWAN is a group of like minded people, forming a community co-operative, established in 2016 to develop and deploy a public Internet of Things, low-power, long range network to businesses, the community and voluntary sector, and the general public.

It was initially fuelled by the energy of Benedict Gaster and Nigel Legg with the aim of building a IoT infastructure for everyone. Since then we have now established a network within Bristol and others have joined, and continue to join, to expand the network and provide their unique skills to the process.
