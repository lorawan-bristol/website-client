Bristol LoRaWAN runs a regular, every other month, meetup for everyone who is interested in LoRaWAN, IoT, and, in particular, Bristol's version of those things.

The meetup is on a Monday and held at the Arnolfini, which is part of UWE's City Campus. More details and registration can be found at http://www.meetup.com/LoRaWAN-Bristol/ .

If your interested in attending check out the site, equally if you got something you want to talk about, just drop us a line.
